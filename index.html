Email Triage

On-device AI that sorts your Gmail inbox — without your email ever leaving your phone.

🔒 Your data stays on your device

Email Triage reads your inbox using your own Google account credentials. No email content is sent to any server owned by the developer. Classification runs on-device using Apple Intelligence.

📱 iOS native

Built in Swift and SwiftUI for iPhone. Uses Apple's on-device AI for classification. Falls back to your own OpenAI or Anthropic API key — never a shared backend.

✅ You confirm before anything changes

Email Triage shows you exactly what it classified and why before applying any labels. Bulk actions require explicit confirmation.
